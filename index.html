Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any cost to me for an estimate?

No. Estimates are completely free with no obligation. Our service costs you nothing — we're compensated by the contractor network, not by homeowners.

How are the roofing contractors in your network vetted?

Every contractor we connect you with is confirmed to carry valid liability insurance, meets Pennsylvania's home improvement contractor registration requirements, and has verifiable local references or completed work we can review before they're added to our network.

How fast will a contractor contact me?

Most homeowners hear back from a local contractor within 24 hours of submitting the form.

What if I'm not happy with the contractor I'm matched with?

Let us know and we'll match you with another vetted contractor in our network at no cost to you.

Do you charge homeowners anything if I hire the contractor?

No. Our matching service is completely free to homeowners, whether or not you decide to hire the contractor we connect you with.
